RSS 2016 Venue

The conference will be held at Rackham Auditorium in Ann Arbor, Michigan, USA.





Conference Banquet

The conference banquet will be hosted at the iconic Henry Ford Museum on Tuesday evening, June 21, 2016. This 12-acre museum features wide-ranging exhibits that document American innovation in agriculture, manufacturing, aviation, automobiles, and more. Transportation to and from the banquet will be provided. You will not want to miss this fantastic event!
